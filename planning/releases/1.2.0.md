# nooka 1.2.0

Your backups, now in the cloud — on your terms. This release adds optional
manual backup and restore to your own Google Drive, plus a quick way to delete
a task you no longer need.

## Highlights

- **Cloud backup to Google Drive.** Settings → Connect Google Drive, then
  **Back up now** to save your whole list to your own Drive. Backups live in a
  hidden per-app folder (the `drive.appdata` space) — invisible to other apps
  and to your main Drive file list. The newest 5 backups are kept automatically.
- **Restore from Drive.** Pick a backup and, after a confirmation, it replaces
  your current list with that snapshot — the same restore behavior as file
  import. Ideal for moving to a new phone.
- **Delete a task.** Remove an active task from its menu, with an undo toast in
  case you change your mind.

## Notes

- Cloud backup is **manual and opt-in** — nothing leaves your device until you
  connect an account and tap Back up now. The app stays local-first by default.
- Restore **replaces** all current data (behind a confirm dialog) rather than
  merging — it's a restore, not a live sync.
