# nooka 1.2.1

A fix release: Google Drive backup and restore now work on Android.

## Fixes

- **Cloud backup/restore on Android.** "Back up now" and "Restore from Drive"
  failed with a generic error right after connecting an account. The silent
  Google authorization call returns nothing on Android even when access was
  just granted; the app now falls back to the standard authorization path, so
  backup and restore complete as intended.

## Notes

- While the app's Google verification is still pending, Android may show a
  "Google hasn't verified this app" screen the first time it requests Drive
  access. Tap **Continue** to proceed.

Sideload the attached `nooka-1.2.1.apk` on Android.
