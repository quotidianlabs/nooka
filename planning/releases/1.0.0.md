# nooka 1.0.0

First public release — a small, fast, local-first to-do list for iOS and
Android. Your data lives in an on-device SQLite database: no account, no
backend, no sync.

## Highlights

- **Category board.** Organize tasks into categories and reorder both tasks and
  categories with drag-and-drop. New tasks remember your last-used category.
- **Finish-and-forget archive.** Completing a task archives it out of your way;
  archived items auto-delete 30 days later.
- **Localized.** Full English and Russian.

Sideload the attached `nooka-1.0.0.apk` on Android.
